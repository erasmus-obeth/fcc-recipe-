Simple Pancake Recipe

This quick pancake recipe makes soft, fluffy pancakes using simple ingredients and takes only a few minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and milk and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and lightly grease it.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden.
